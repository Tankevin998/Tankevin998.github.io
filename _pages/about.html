--- permalink: / layout: archive title: "About me" excerpt: "About me" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /about/ - /about.html --- I am a research associate intern in the Department of Computer Science, Vanderbilt University, advised by Prof. Thomas Beckers. Before this, I received M.S. in Electrical Engineering at Washington University in St.Louis. I received B.Eng in Software Engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University in 2019 and M.S.E in Robotics at GRASP Lab, University of Pennsylvania in 2021. I was an exchange student at SungKyunKwan University in 2018. The name of this webpage "wonggwan" comes from the pronunciation of my name in Cantonese. I can speak English and Korean fluently while being a native speaker in Cantonese and Mandarin Chinese. My research interests include machine learning, control, and robotics.
