--- permalink: / layout: archive title: "About me" excerpt: "About me" author_profile: true redirect_from: - /about/ - /about.html --- I am a first-year PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis under the supervision of Prof. Yiannis Kantaros. Previously, I spent 7 months as a research intern in Robotics and Sensor Physics department at Schlumberger-Doll Research. I graduated with my Master's degree in Robotics at GRASP Lab, University of Pennsylvania in 2021, where I was working on Robust Semantic Segmantation under the supervision of Prof. George Pappas and Hamed Hassani. I graduated with my Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University in 2019. I was an exchange student at SungKyunKwan University in 2018. The name of this webpage "wonggwan" comes from the pronunciation of my name in Cantonese. I can speak English and Korean fluently while being a native speaker in Cantonese and Mandarin Chinese.
